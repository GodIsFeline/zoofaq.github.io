Terms

Bestiality and Beastiality

  • Bestiality refers exclusively to the act of intercourse with animals.[0][1]
  • Beastialty is simply a common misspelling of Bestiality, and isn't actually a word.[2]

Zoophilia and Zoosexuality

Zoophilia and Zoosexuality, depending on who you ask, are varying degrees of interchangable. In their most unique definitions:

  • Zoophilia is the attraction, in any form, to animals[3]
  • Zoosexuality is the attraction to animals romantically (and optionally sexually)

When in the context of discussions with non-zoophiles, zoosexuality is rarely used, while zoophilia retains its meaning.
